The Experience

A Journey of Choice

Wanderer Wheels is a story-driven open-world experience set in a modest countryside. You play as a husband traveling in a converted van that serves as a mobile trading shop.

Behind the calm journey lies a personal motivation. Your wife is ill, and her ongoing treatment depends on the choices you make. This isn't about fast profits—it's about emotional weight.

Drive, trade, and converse. Slowly get to know the people and places along the road in a compact world designed for a thoughtful 3–4 hour playthrough.

Features

Life On The Road

Van Life

Drive a converted van, manage your mobile inventory, and trade everyday items with villagers in remote locations.

Living World

Meet locals, take on side jobs, and engage in quiet conversations that reveal the soul of the community.

Meaningful Stakes

Your choices impact your wife's treatment. Every detour and trade carries emotional weight, not just financial gain.
